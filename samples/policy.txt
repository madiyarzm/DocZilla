Acme Corp Vendor Contract Compliance Policy

Data Retention

All customer data must be retained for a minimum of seven (7) years and must be
encrypted at rest using industry-standard encryption (AES-256 or stronger).

Data Deletion

Vendors must delete or return all customer personal data within thirty (30) days
of a written deletion request, in accordance with GDPR.

Termination

Either party may terminate the agreement, but only with at least thirty (30)
days of prior written notice to the other party.

Confidentiality

All confidential information must remain protected, and confidentiality
obligations must survive termination of the agreement for at least three (3)
years.

Liability

The contract must include a limitation of liability clause that caps each
party's aggregate liability at the total fees paid under the agreement.

Governing Law

The agreement must specify the governing law and jurisdiction for disputes.

Subprocessors

The vendor must obtain prior written approval before engaging any third-party
subprocessor that will handle customer data.
