Master Services Agreement between Acme Corp ("Client") and DataWorks LLC ("Vendor")

1. Data Retention

The Vendor shall retain Client data for a period of three (3) years following
the end of the engagement, after which the data may be archived or deleted at
the Vendor's discretion.

2. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement by providing thirty (30) days prior
written notice to the other party.

3. Confidentiality

Each party agrees to protect the other party's confidential information using
reasonable care. These confidentiality obligations shall survive the
termination of this Agreement for a period of three (3) years.

4. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of
the State of Delaware, and the parties consent to the exclusive jurisdiction of
its courts.

5. Subprocessors

The Vendor may engage third-party subprocessors as needed to deliver the
services.

6. General

This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties and
supersedes all prior agreements.
